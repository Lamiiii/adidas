New arrivals for men

Check out adidas’ new arrivals for men. Discover the latest in menswear with new tops, tees, tracksuits, footwear, and more. Whether you need new pieces for sports training, the gym, or simply want to look good on the street, you’ll find it in adidas’ new arrivals for men.

Apparel for all occasions

adidas has been a leader in sportswear for decades, and its legacy lives on through the iconic 3-Stripes and dedication to high quality. Be part of the legend with new tees, tops, tracksuits, and more. Available in a wide range of colors and styles, and with regular, wide and slim fits, there’s something to suite everyone. And the new arrivals for men don’t just look good – leading technologies such as Climacool and Climalite will keep you dry and comfortable while you work out or play. Mesh linings and UV and wind protection can be found in selected apparel to further optimize your comfort and performance.

Choose from shoes that are designed to optimize sports performance or classic streetwear items with limited-edition colors or patterns. Available in a variety fabrics with metallics, pastels, and embroidery, men’s footwear comes in full and half sizes for a perfect streamlined fit. Choose to stand out with the new arrivals for men.
